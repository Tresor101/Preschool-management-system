Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly!

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

How do I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling us at (555) 123-KIDS, emailing admissions@littlestars.edu, or filling out our contact form above. Tours are available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

What age groups do you accept?

We welcome children from 6 months to 5 years old. We have specialized programs for infants (6-18 months), toddlers (18 months - 2 years), preschoolers (3-4 years), and pre-K students (4-5 years).

Do you offer part-time programs?

Yes! We offer both full-time and part-time options to accommodate different family needs. Part-time options include 2-day, 3-day, and morning-only programs.

What should I bring for enrollment?

Please bring your child's immunization records, birth certificate, emergency contact information, and any relevant medical information. We'll provide you with our enrollment packet during your tour.

Do you provide meals and snacks?

Yes, we provide healthy, nutritious meals and snacks daily. Our menu is designed by a pediatric nutritionist and accommodates various dietary restrictions and allergies.
